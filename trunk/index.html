Bacon

Bacon may be eaten smoked, boiled, fried, baked, or grilled, or used as a minor ingredient to flavor dishes. Bacon is also used for barding and larding roasts, especially game birds. The word is derived from the Old High German bacho, meaning "buttock", "ham" or "side of bacon".

Arena

The Arena browser predated Netscape Navigator and Microsoft's Internet Explorer and featured a number innovations which were later used in other commercial products. It was the first browser to support background images, tables, text flow around images, and inline mathematical expressions.
